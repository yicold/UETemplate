How to use this template

Click to open storyboard template in the Pages panel. Once there:

1. Name your storyboard

2. Double click to change background image. You can scan in and add your own sketches.

3. Describe the actions going on in the scene

4. This iPhone was created using Axure's 8 drawing tool, you can draw your own or import foreground elements using the image widget

5. Use the snapshot widget to include your wireframe. In Publish mode you can click on the wireframe to launch your prototype right from your storyboard!

6. This print crop mark ensures your storyboard prints on one page.
